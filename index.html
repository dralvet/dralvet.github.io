Microsoft Marketplace Agent

AI Auditor

AI Auditor is a comprehensive tenant discovery and assessment agent that evaluates AI agent readiness and Responsible AI compliance across the Microsoft Cloud ecosystem.

It runs wide, Graph-powered assessments across Microsoft Entra ID, Microsoft 365, Intune, Teams, SharePoint, Exchange, Microsoft Defender, Microsoft Purview, and additional Microsoft Graph-connected services exposed through the Microsoft Graph API.

Mission

Provide organizations with a clear, evidence-based readiness picture for AI agents and Copilot deployments by continuously assessing security posture, governance maturity, privacy controls, and compliance alignment.

What AI Auditor Assesses

Security Baseline
  • Identity posture, access control, and conditional access coverage
  • Endpoint and workload protection signals from Defender and Intune
  • Privilege, exposure, and policy gaps that impact AI deployment risk
Data Governance
  • Data classification, sensitivity labeling, and retention controls
  • Purview governance and lifecycle readiness for AI-accessible content
  • Oversharing, sprawl, and permission hygiene across collaboration services
Privacy & Compliance
  • Privacy-by-design and access-minimization readiness checks
  • Control mapping to regulatory and internal policy requirements
  • Operational guardrails for safe and auditable AI adoption

Responsible AI Alignment

AI Auditor evaluates your environment against Microsoft Responsible AI principles and highlights practical remediations for each domain:

  • Fairness: identify policy and data access conditions that can bias outcomes.
  • Reliability & Safety: assess resilience controls and safe operational boundaries.
  • Privacy & Security: verify protection of sensitive data and least-privilege access.
  • Inclusiveness: surface collaboration and accessibility gaps impacting adoption.
  • Transparency: promote traceability through evidence-backed findings and reporting.
  • Accountability: connect ownership, controls, and remediation workflows.

Business Outcomes

  • Accelerate secure Copilot and AI agent rollouts with confidence.
  • Prioritize remediation with tenant-wide visibility and risk context.
  • Demonstrate Responsible AI readiness to leadership, audit, and compliance teams.
